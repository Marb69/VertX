About VertX

You have successfully installed vertX on your system! Now you can begin working with the powerful tools and features it offers. For more details, check out our documentation, FAQ section, or quick start guides to help you get up and running smoothly.

vertX is designed for both development and educational use. It comes with optimized tools to help you build projects faster while learning the core technologies behind them. Please note that if you're planning to make vertX accessible online, ensure you review our security tips and best practices to protect your setup.

Start the vertX Dashboard to view your current status and manage your environment.


Community

vertX is supported by a growing developer community. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced developer, there's always something new to learn or share. Join the discussion on our Forums, subscribe to our Newsletter, follow us on social media, or contribute to our blog to stay updated.
